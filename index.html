Biography

Dr. Jichuan Zeng (in Chinese: 曾纪川), is currently a senior research engineer at ByteDance. He received his Ph.D. degree in Computer Science and Engineering from the Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK) at 2019, supervised by Prof. Irwin King and Prof. Michael R. Lyu. Before CUHK, he was a research assistant at Rich Media and Big Data Analytics Key Laboratory of Shenzhen. He received his Master degree in Computer Science and Technology from Harbin Institute of Technology (Shenzhen) supervised by Prof. Qingcai Chen in 2014, and Bachelor degree in Educational Information Technology from South China Normal University in 2010.

His research interests include natural language processing, machine learning, recommendation system and software engineering.
